Hi, I'm Nachiketa Patil

MS by Research Student at CMInDS, IIT Bombay

I work with Prof. Arjun Bhagoji, and my research interests are Computer Vision and Adversarial Machine Learning.

This site is where I share my research, projects, and other interesting things I come across during my journey. Thanks for stopping by!
